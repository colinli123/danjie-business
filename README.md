## Overview

Danjie Business is a comprehensive skill that distills wisdom from 10 of the world's most influential business leaders. From Lei Jun's "Ride the Wind" philosophy to Buffett's value investing, Musk's first principles, and Thiel's zero-to-one thinking—this skill provides actionable frameworks for strategic decision-making, investment analysis, and business building.

## Core Features

- **10 Business Masters' Wisdom**: In-depth analysis of thinking models from China, global investment giants, and Silicon Valley tech leaders
- **6 Knowledge Modules**: Business essence, thinking models, industry insights, life wisdom, decision-making frameworks, and case studies
- **Practical Applications**: Clear guidance on when and how to apply each principle
